<h4 class="alert-heading">Problem 68</h4>

If $f(x) = x + 4$ and $h(x) = 4x - 1$, find a function $g$ such that $g \circ f = h$.

</div>

<div class="alert alert-success" role="alert">
<h4 class="alert-heading">Solution</h4>

$$
\begin{aligned}
h(x) &= (g \circ f)(x) = g(f(x)) \\
4x - 1 &= g(x + 4) \\
4(x+4)-17 &= g(x + 4) \\
4t-17 &= g(t)
\end{aligned}
$$

Therefore, $g(x) = 4x-17$

</div>
